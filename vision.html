Vision

  1. Home
  2. Pages
  3. Vision

TRIOA will establish an institution that serves as a model for reentry programs nationwide featuring quality housing units that provide a safe and comfortable living environment for TRIOA's residents. The community will be designed on principles of respect, responsibility, social interaction and relationship building, while fostering a sense of belonging among the residents. The institution will also include a “strip-mall like'' series of storefronts that will be occupied by America's leading industries who are committed to training TRIOA's residents. The storefronts will offer a range of services, including job training, educational opportunities, and resources for starting and running small businesses. Residents will also receive support in developing critical life skills, such as communication, and financial literacy to ensure they can successfully navigate the challenges of self-sufficiency. TRIOA's community will be a vibrant and supportive environment that fosters growth, development, and leadership.
